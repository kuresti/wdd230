News Release

In local news, there was a family of squirrels saved by a young local boy named Timmy Jones. The mother squirrel suffered a broken leg and Timmy hand fed the baby squirrels until the mother squirrel's leg was healed. Local veterinarian Agatha Smith pronounced Timmy a hero at the last Desert Chamber of Commerce meeting. Mother and babies were successfully returned to their natural habitat.



In other news. A big thank you to the Happy Movers moving company for assisting Cookies 'R' Us as they moved to their new location on Pitch Lane.
