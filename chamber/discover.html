Desert Mountain

Desert Mountain, a small town with something for everyone. Established as a mining town in 1847 this town has kept its old town charm while expanding its horizons to becoming a tourist destination year round. With a local population of 10,000 this town is big enough to provide top of the line service, yet still keep its small town charm and hospitality. Come discover this hidden gem nestled in a beautiful mountain valley.

Pioneer Park

A town hertitage site created to honor the pioneer men and women who first settled the area. This beautiful park includes peaceful walking paths, playgrounds, picnic areas, and an ampitheatre to enjoy local outdoor concerts in the cool summer evenings.

Shakespeare Garden

In honor of the great bard the local community college has cultivated a beautiful Shakespearean garden. This is a favorite location for weddings and romantic evening strolls.

Mountain Heights Balloon Festival

Join us this year in celebrating the 75th year of hosting This event. There will be great food, activities for all ages, and of course colorful balloons from around the country.

Winter Wonderland

Enjoy world class skiing on powder from which dreams are made. Visitors and locals alike also enjoy other winter activities including, snowmobiling, crosscountry skiing, snowboarding, and skijumping.

Monthly Lumberjack Celebration

Come be apart of our montly celebration where we celebrate all things Lumberjack. Meet our mascot "Flap-Jack". Events include sunrise breakfast with a flap-jack tossing contest, log rolling, best beaerd contest, log cutting, most creative maple syrup confection, and many more. Join in your best Lumberjack shirt for a full day of fun!
