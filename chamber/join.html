Membership Levels

Non-Profit Level

  • Yearly Fee: None
  • Live social media interview with Chamber President annually.
  • Member spotlight on website annually.
  • Free attendance at Chamber-sponsored events.
  • Access to member discounts.
  • Access to webinar library.

Bronze Level

  • Yearly Fee: $365
  • All the benefits of non-profit membership plus...
  • Table sponsor at networking event annually.
  • Free professional seminars and educational presentations annually.
  • Ribbon cutton or Open House annually.

Silver Level

  • Yearly Fee: $600
  • All the benefits of bronze membership plus...
  • Regular job postings on the Chamber website.
  • Sponsor of one major event annually.
  • Logo on the Chamber newsletter quarterly.
  • Host a Chamber after hours event bi-annually.

Gold Level

  • Yearly Fee: $1000
  • All the benefits of silver membership plus...
  • Host/teach a seminar, webinar or event quarterly.
  • Sponsor of one major event annually.
  • Contribute an article in the Chamber social media newsletter monthly.
  • Sponsorship of a Chamber Games team.
  • A promotional benefits packages is also included.
