Our History

Bountiful Foods has grown from a small family owned business selling high quality, customized fruit drinks along with fresh fruit and produce from a food truck, to one of the most popular fruit and produce companies in Carlsbad, California.

Bountiful Foods owes its success to being committed to sourcing its fruit from organic local farmers. Our produce is the freshest and most flavorful because it is free from harmful pesticides and chemicals. We also work with international suppliers who practice sustainable farming in order to provide a greater variety of fruits to our customers.

Carlsbad

Carlsbad is located in North San Diego County, California. It is a city of beautiful beaches and family-friendly attractions. Visitors from all over the world come to enjoy its mild climate and laid-back lifestyle.

This coastal city is an ideal location for outdoor activities with several parks and nature preserves in the area to explore. Along with water sports such as surfing and kayaking.

Bountiful Foods has become an integral part of the Carlsbad community, providing locals and tourists alike with their daily dose of fresh, healthy produce. The company's commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing has made them a favorite among envronmentally conscious consumers.
