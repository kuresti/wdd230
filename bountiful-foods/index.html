Encinitas Farming

Encinitas farmers are mostly small-scale farmers that focus on sustainable, organic, and local food production. Here in the mild climate of California the soil is fertile and well-suited for growing a variety of crops that include fruits, vegetables, and herbs. This area is known for its specialization in rare organic heirloom varieties rarely found in large supermarkets. The main vehicle of sale for these farmers is local farmer's markets and Bountiful Foods. Both provide popular destinations for residents and visitors alike, offering fresh produce and fruit. Some area farmers offer tours and farm-to-table dining experiences that teach visitors about sustainable farming while supporting local farmers.
