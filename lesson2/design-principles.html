PARC: Alignment

Walmart
www.walmart.com

The site for Walmart makes good use of alignment. The circle images are aligned both vertically aligned horizontally. The text below the images is also aligned vertically at the top and horizontally. This use of alignment gives the site a clean, organized look.

White Space and Clean Design

Squarespace
www.squarespace.com

The use of white space on the Squarespace westie gives the page a clean, uncluttered look. The space between elements, along with text alignment lead the eye to the call to action button and is very effective.

PARC: Contrast

United Health Care
www.uhc.com

On this site the use of dark blud font over a white background provides a simple, yet effective contrast. Constrast can also be found in the colors of the women's shirts against the dark blue lines of the image at the top of the page. The usage of the dark blue on white contrast works well in establishing heirarchy and guiding the eye to the call to action button.
